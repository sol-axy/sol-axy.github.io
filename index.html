Overview of Solana and Its Challenges

High-Speed Blockchain: Solana is known for its high throughput and low transaction costs, making it a popular choice for developers and users.

Introduction to Solaxy

Solaxy aims to mitigate these issues by providing a Layer-2 solution that processes transactions off the main chain. This approach not only alleviates congestion but also enhances the overall user experience on the Solana network.
